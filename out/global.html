draw()

Displays walls and the source particle To automatically move the source around, we introdiuce Perlin noise with noise(). We provide the Perlin noise function with an offset argument, i.e. xoff and then yoff, and we multiply them by the width and height of the canvas, respectively. We then update our particle coordinates with our new x and y.
